about me

I've been learning web development for the last few years after being inspired by experiences with Processing and Java. I wanted to write code but I also needed a suitable medium through which to share what I've written. Since learning to design and develop the web on my own time and through classes, I've taken jobs as a freelancer, worked at a small design studio creating responsive sites for a variety of clients, experimented with tools like Ionic and Cordova to create native apps, and now I create large web applications with Meteor. I pride myself in being an autodidact, and I enjoy playing with new technologies like VR, or learning new tools to develop with, like Python and Django.

What I look forward to most in my development career is working with a small, dedicated team to tackle a diverse range of challenges, ranging from websites to apps, and beyond. I find that I work excellently in a team environment, because I am afforded more time to ensure that what we create is the absolute best it can be.

I worked on both the front and back ends, and have been the primary developer on the project since I started it. This application has garnered national interest, and we are in the process of organizing the first pilot tests in several states.

Due to the competitive nature of the service, and it being in alpha, I am unable to show the app in full, but I'd love to discuss the process of creating it so far, and give a demonstration of what we are doing.
