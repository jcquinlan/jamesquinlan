james quinlan - app designer

I decided to become involved with Android app development after experimenting with Java and Processing. I ended up partnering with a Java developer to build our first app, entitled Murmur. I was inspired by the flocking of starlings as a defensive manuever and thought that to control a flock would be an interesting game mechanic for our first application. I was also inspired by cellular biology, in which the circle of life is simplified; kill or be killed. These are a few of the rough mockups that I have made pre-release of v1.0.
